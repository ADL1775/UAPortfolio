This is a simple "Adventure Game".
The objective is to find all of the items.

ITEMS:
Comb
Brush
Bowl
Soap
Vase
Candlestick


** COMMANDS **
Navigation:
N = Go north
S = Go south
E = Go east
W = Go west

L = Display your location
I = Display your inventory

G <itemName> = Pick up an item.
D <itemName> = Drop an item from your inventory.
